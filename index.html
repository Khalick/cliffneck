About Cliffneck

Located in the vibrant town of Kerugoya, Cliffneck offers an unforgettable dining experience with authentic cuisine crafted from the finest local ingredients.

Our chefs blend traditional recipes with modern culinary techniques to create dishes that delight your senses and warm your heart.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual meal with loved ones, Cliffneck is your destination for exceptional food and hospitality.
